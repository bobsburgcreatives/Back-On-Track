DIABETES WELLNESS RETREAT

A life-changing experience designed for individuals with Type 2 Diabetes (T2D), located on the stunning Aegean Sea coast in Platamonas, Greece, just 25 km from Mount Olympus.

At Back on Track, our program recognizes the physical, psychological, and social factors as deeply interconnected in both the manifestation of symptoms and our relationship to them.

Our team goes beyond medical treatment; we are committed to building strong relationships with participants, fostering a community environment where individuals feel supported and cared for and can explore the physical, emotional, and social impacts of T2D.

An Interdisciplinary Approach for Better Outcomes


Our approach allows us to come closer to our participants.

Our approach allows us to come closer to our participants.
By creating an environment where participants join a community, we manage to strip away the distant medical model of care.
We are guided by evidence and research-based practices, yet we remain keenly aware that each participant is unique, ensuring that our approach is not reduced to another mechanical process of care. This philosophy is supported by numerous recent studies on the benefits of a multidisciplinary approach, which highlight the holistic impacts of integrated care. It is well documented that combining medical treatment, nutritional counseling, psychological support, and physical activity significantly enhances patient outcomes, emphasizing personalized, comprehensive health management.

What We Offer

Our program integrates expert medical care, psychological support, and lifestyle modifications, all delivered in a peaceful community setting where participants can feel safe and supported.

Medical
Consultations

Participants undergo assessments and receive personalized guidance from our endocrinologist and diabetes nurse.
Psychological
Support

Individual psychotherapy sessions with a combination of group psychotherapeutic interventions.
Interdisciplinary Workshops

Participants learn about effective lifestyle changes, including dietary planning, fitness routines, and stress management techniques.
Cultural and Recreational Activities

In addition to medical and psychological care, our retreat includes fun and enriching activities like seaside trips, and cultural experiences.
